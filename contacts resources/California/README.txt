Resources used to make infer the mixing patterns in the household, work, community, and school layers for California.

household_distribution_CA: propOrtion of the White, Black, Asian, and Latino populations that live in households size 1-6+. Absolute mean gives average household size for each population. 
Source: https://dof.ca.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/352/Reports/Demographic_Reports/documents/CACPS07_final.pdf 

public_school_demographics_CA: the number of kids between the ages of 5-17 (as reported throuh the 2021 census) enrolled in public schools in each major racial/ethnic group. Note, Alpine County is censored for small population size.
